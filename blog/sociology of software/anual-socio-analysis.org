#+DESCRIPTION: Overview of the social aspects of software niche

On January 15, 2018 Stack Overflow published the results of the [[https://insights.stackoverflow.com/survey/2018/?utm_source=Iterable&utm_medium=email&utm_campaign=dev-survey-2018-promotion][annual
survey]] conducted in January.

The word "developer" will be exclusively used as an abstraction for
the average developer who responded to this year Stack Overflow's
survey.

* Over 50 percent of developers are from university-attended families (Bachelor's + Master's)
  There are likely to be categorized as mid-class citizens in the US, using this criterion.

* Almost 70 percent of developers are between 25

* Above 90 percent of developers are male

* Above 70 percent of developers are white or of European descent

* Over 90 percent of developers are of straight or heterosexual orientation

* Over 70 percent of developers have no children and any other dependents

* Over 50 percent of developers spend between 9 and 12 hours coding

* Only miserable 12.5 percent of developers go out more than two hours a day

* Over 35 percent of developers do not exercise at all
  Presumably, they don't practice any other physical activity during
  their working day.

* Global median salary is $70,000

* Almost 70 percent of developers are satisfied

* Vague apprehension of ethics
  The eluding of responsibility. Upper management is accounted for the
  ethical transgressions.
